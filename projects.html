Rock Paper Scissors Lizards Spock or RPSLS for short is a game
simulation where you can play a game of RPSLS against an AI, a Friend,
or have an AI vs AI game with randomly generated names for each player.

Lemonade Stand is a recreation of the classic game on CoolMathGames
where you run your own lemonade stand and try and make as much money as
possible while balancing your budget and dealing with costumer satisfaction.

Robots vs Dinosaurs is a battle simulation between a fleet of Robots and
a herd of Dinosaurs where you choose certain dinosaurs and robots to attack
until one sides forcers are completetly depleted.
